## Primary Activities
1. **Inbound Logistics**: Receiving, storing, distributing inputs
2. **Operations**: Transforming inputs into final product
3. **Outbound Logistics**: Delivering product to customers
4. **Marketing & Sales**: Attracting and acquiring customers
5. **Service**: After-sale support, retention

## Support Activities
1. **Firm Infrastructure**: Management, planning, finance, legal
2. **Human Resource Management**: Recruiting, training, compensation
3. **Technology Development**: R&D, IT systems, automation
4. **Procurement**: Purchasing inputs, vendor management

## Analysis Steps
1. Identify each activity in the value chain
2. Assess cost and value contribution of each
3. Identify links between activities
4. Find opportunities for optimization
5. Compare with competitors' value chains

# Blue Ocean Strategy - ERRC Grid
# Tham khảo: INSEAD, "Blue Ocean Strategy" (Kim & Mauborgne)

## Concept
Tạo uncontested market space thay vì cạnh tranh trong Red Ocean (bloody competition).

## ERRC Grid (Four Actions Framework)

### Eliminate (Loại bỏ)
- Yếu tố nào ngành vẫn làm nhưng không cần thiết?
- What factors that the industry takes for granted should be eliminated?

### Reduce (Giảm)
- Yếu tố nào nên giảm xuống dưới chuẩn ngành?
- What factors should be reduced well below the industry standard?

### Raise (Tăng)
- Yếu tố nào nên nâng cao hơn chuẩn ngành?
- What factors should be raised well above the industry standard?

### Create (Tạo mới)
- Yếu tố nào ngành chưa từng offer?
- What factors should be created that the industry has never offered?

## Strategy Canvas
- X axis: Key competing factors
- Y axis: Offering level (Low → High)
- Plot industry average curve vs your value curve
- Goal: Value curve khác biệt rõ ràng

## Six Paths Framework
1. Look across alternative industries
2. Look across strategic groups
3. Look across buyer groups
4. Look across complementary offerings
5. Look across functional-emotional appeal
6. Look across time

## Quadrants
- **Market Penetration** (Existing Product, Existing Market): Lowest risk, increase share
- **Market Development** (Existing Product, New Market): Expand geographically or new segments  
- **Product Development** (New Product, Existing Market): Innovation for current customers
- **Diversification** (New Product, New Market): Highest risk, new business areas

## Application
- Determine growth strategy priority
- Assess risk level of each option
- Balance portfolio of growth initiatives

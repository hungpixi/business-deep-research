## Quadrants
- **Stars** (High Growth, High Share): Invest heavily, future cash cows
- **Cash Cows** (Low Growth, High Share): Milk for cash, maintain
- **Question Marks** (High Growth, Low Share): Invest or divest decision
- **Dogs** (Low Growth, Low Share): Divest or harvest

## Application
- Plot product lines / business units
- Allocate resources accordingly
- Balance portfolio between quadrants

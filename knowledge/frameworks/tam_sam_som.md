# TAM/SAM/SOM Market Sizing
# Tham khảo: VC/Startup standard, Harvard Business School

## TAM (Total Addressable Market)
- Toàn bộ thị trường nếu chiếm 100%
- Top-down: Industry reports, Statista, etc.
- Bottom-up: Total potential customers x Annual revenue per customer

## SAM (Serviceable Available Market)
- Phần thị trường product có thể phục vụ (geography, segment, channel)
- TAM filtered by: geographic reach, language, regulations, product fit

## SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
- Phần thực tế có thể chiếm trong 1-3 năm
- SAM x realistic market share % (usually 1-5% first 3 years)
- Based on: marketing budget, sales capacity, competitive landscape

## Calculation Methods
1. **Top-Down**: Start from industry size → filter down
2. **Bottom-Up**: # of potential customers x average revenue per customer
3. **Value Theory**: Value delivered x willingness to pay x addressable customers

## Validation
- Cross-reference with competitor revenues
- Triangulate top-down vs bottom-up
- Sanity check: SOM should be achievable with current resources

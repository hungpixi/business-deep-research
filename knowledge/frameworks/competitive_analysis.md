# Competitive Analysis Framework
# Tham khảo: Marketing Management (Kotler), HBS Competitive Strategy

## I. Competitor Identification

### Direct Competitors
- Cùng sản phẩm/dịch vụ, cùng target customer
- Market share estimation

### Indirect Competitors
- Khác sản phẩm nhưng giải quyết cùng vấn đề
- Alternative solutions

### Potential Competitors
- Có thể enter market trong tương lai
- Adjacent players, big tech

## II. Competitor Profiling

Cho mỗi đối thủ chính (top 3-5), phân tích:

### Company Overview
- Founding year, size, funding
- Revenue estimate (if available)
- Growth trajectory

### Product/Service
- Core features
- Pricing model
- Technology stack
- USP (Unique Selling Point)

### Market Position
- Target segments
- Market share %
- Brand perception
- Geographic coverage

### Strengths & Weaknesses
- Competitive advantages
- Known limitations/gaps
- Customer complaints

## IV. Positioning Map
- X axis: Price (Low → High)
- Y axis: Quality/Features (Basic → Premium)
- Plot all competitors
- Identify white space / opportunity

## V. Competitive Advantage Assessment
1. **Cost Leadership**: Có thể offer giá thấp hơn?
2. **Differentiation**: Feature/service nào unique?
3. **Focus/Niche**: Segment nào chưa được serve tốt?
4. **First-Mover**: Có advantage timing không?
5. **Network Effects**: User base tạo value loop không?

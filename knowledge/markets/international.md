# Thị Trường Quốc Tế - Market Context

## US Market
- GDP: ~$28T, mature digital economy
- SaaS market: $300B+, highly competitive
- VC funding: ~$170B/year
- Regulatory: SEC, GDPR (EU), CCPA, SOX
- Go-to-market: PLG, enterprise sales, content marketing

## EU Market
- GDPR compliance mandatory
- Diverse languages and cultures
- Strong sustainability/ESG focus
- Digital Single Market initiative

## Global Expansion Considerations
- Localization (language, currency, culture)
- Regulatory compliance per country
- Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal, local gateways)
- Customer support timezone coverage
- Tax implications (transfer pricing, permanent establishment)
- IP protection across jurisdictions

# Thị Trường Đông Nam Á (SEA) - Market Context

## Overview
- Population: ~700 million
- Digital economy: $200B+ (2025)
- Key markets: Indonesia, Vietnam, Thailand, Philippines, Singapore, Malaysia

## ASEAN Trade Integration
- AFTA (ASEAN Free Trade Area): Thuế 0-5%
- RCEP: Free trade với China, Japan, Korea, Australia, NZ
- CPTPP: Vietnam member, higher standards

## Digital Economy Trends
- Super apps (Grab, GoTo, Sea Group)
- E-commerce boom (Shopee, Lazada, Tokopedia)
- Fintech/digital banking growing fast
- AI adoption accelerating
